Stephen Costigan's Semester Experience Summary:

I think this semester was a good one for learning. I only had a basic grasp on CSS/JSON coming into the semester and so was able to benefit from the understanding of my classmates.

This project began as a more ambitious affair, but after losing Julio, who was our guy for SQL incorporation, we had to take a hard look at our project. Missy had done PHP in the past, but this was too much for us to make happen and so had to simplify the entire thing.

I think this was a positive thing in the end. Sometimes, you have to understand your own limitations and work within your abilities. Since neither of us was able to properly secure the assignment with encryption, we had to completely put it aside and work on what the semester covered.

All things considered, it was a great semester for learning.
